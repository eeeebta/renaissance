Exploring Beyonce's Renaissance

Beyonce's RENAISSANCE album, released on July 29, 2022, is her seventh studio album to date. Heavily inspired by Black and Queer electronic, hip-hop, and disco music, RENAISSANCE is an hour-long journey of self-expression, escapism, and liberation. ACT III: A Subjective and (Brief) Narrative Analysis

In addition to the musical complexity of the album, RENAISSANCE touches a wide range of "feeling" aspects. Its combination of high danceablity, balance of speech and melody, and evocation of being on dancefloor keep the listener engaged and interested. The lyrics are unapologetic and celebratory, resulting in a euphoric and liberating album experience.
